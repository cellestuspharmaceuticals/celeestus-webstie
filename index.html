About Us

Cellestus Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd. is backed by over 20 years of experience in manufacturing and marketing by its founder. Since its incorporation in 2014, the company has been growing steadily and aims to expand its presence across India and beyond.

As an emerging global pharmaceutical company, we have proven expertise in product research, manufacturing, and marketing. Over the years, Cellestus Pharmaceuticals has grown into one of India's fastest-growing pharmaceutical firms, with a strong focus on specialty segments and international expansion.
